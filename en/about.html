About the Project

This resume builder was created to help anyone quickly create a professional resume online and download it as a PDF without registration or complicated settings.

Our Goal

We want to make resume creation as simple and convenient as possible. Fill in your information, choose a suitable template, and get a ready-to-use document in just a few minutes.

What Is Already Available

  • Create resumes online
  • Upload a photo
  • Multiple resume templates
  • Download as PDF
  • No registration required

Project Development

The resume builder is actively being developed. We regularly improve existing templates and add new features.

  • New resume templates
  • Improved design of existing templates
  • Additional customization options
  • New tools for easier resume creation

Feedback
